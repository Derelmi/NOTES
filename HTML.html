DEPRESSION? a crazy word we all use to describe our state of complete downess{in my own words}. Depression is when everything feels too hard, When you feel so low that things you previously enjoyed no longer hold that same joy.

How do I know I am feeling depressed? Depression is a serious mental illness that can interfere with a person's life. It can cause long-lasting and severe feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a loss of interest in activities. It can also cause physical symptoms of pain, appetite changes, and sleep problems.

  •  Family history;Though there are no specific genes that we can look at and trace to depression, if your family members have had depression, you are more likely also to experience depression.
